Section 02
Key CRO Findings
Three high-severity gaps identified during live crawl — all addressable without checkout modifications or deep discounting, per brief constraints.
● High Severity
BNPL Invisible on PDP
Klarna and Zippay are both active — confirmed via footer links — but zero BNPL messaging appears anywhere on the PDP buybox. For a $50–$150 swimwear range, instalment messaging is proven to reduce payment friction and lift ATC rates by 13%+.
▶#9658; PDP · Buybox
● High Severity
Zero Trust Signals at Point of Purchase
The cart page shows three trust icons — "Great International Shipping Rates," "Exchanges and Returns," "Call Our Bikini Specialists" — yet none of these appear on the PDP where the purchase decision is actually made. These reassurances need to be at the ATC zone.
▶#9658; PDP · ATC Zone
● Medium Severity
"Sale Price" Label on Every Product — No Actual Discount
Shopify's products.json confirms compare_at_price: null on all inspected variants — meaning no real discount exists — yet the theme displays "Sale price" as the default price label on all PLP cards. This erodes brand trust and dilutes the impact of genuine future promotions.
▶#9658; PLP · Product Cards

Fixing the theme bug that displays "Sale price" on all PLP product cards — when no actual compare-at price exists — will improve browsing trust and increase PLP-to-PDP click-through rate by removing false urgency signals that erode credibility with informed shoppers.

Implementation Notes
Shopify Liquid · product-card snippet
  • In the product card snippet (typically product-card.liquid or card-product.liquid), find the price label block and wrap the "Sale price" text in a conditional: {% if product.compare_at_price > product.price %}
  • When compare_at_price is null or equal to price, render "Regular price" (or suppress the label entirely — test both variants separately if bandwidth allows)
  • The existing "Sale price" appears to be the theme's default price label, not a dynamic conditional — a one-line Liquid fix resolves this without breaking genuine sale display
  • GrowthBook flag: plp-price-label-fix — can be implemented as a direct theme fix rather than A/B test if team prefers shipping a fix; otherwise 50/50 on collection pages
  • Primary metric: PLP click-through rate · Secondary: overall CVR

Section 05
Recommended Next Steps
Suggested 6-week implementation and testing roadmap.
Week 1–2
Instrument & Baseline
Set up GrowthBook SDK on Shopify, define experiment metrics in analytics (ATC rate, CVR, revenue per session). Establish 2-week baseline to confirm funnel benchmarks before any changes go live.
Week 2
Launch Test 03 — PLP Price Label Fix
Quickest implementation: single Liquid conditional in the product card snippet. Can ship as a direct fix or 50/50 A/B test. Expected stat-sig in 7–10 days given 406K monthly sessions on collection pages.
Week 3
Launch Test 02 — PDP Trust Strip
2–4 hours dev work. Deploy the trust strip below the ATC button on PDP using existing copy from the cart page. Run 50/50 on all PDP traffic. Primary metric: CVR on PDP; secondary: ATC-to-checkout rate.
Week 4
Launch Test 01 — BNPL Buybox Messaging
Integrate Klarna on-site widget + Zippay inline widget below the price on PDP. Dynamic instalment calculation via Liquid. 50/50 split on PDP visitors. Highest revenue potential of the three tests.
Week 5–6
Read Results & Ship Winners
With 406K monthly sessions, most tests will reach statistical significance within 2–3 weeks. Winners ship to 100% of traffic. Stage 2 audit to identify next tier of opportunities (PDP reviews, homepage personalisation, cart upsell).
