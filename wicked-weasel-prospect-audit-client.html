Section 02
Key CRO Findings
Three high-severity gaps identified during site analysis — all addressable without checkout modifications or deep discounting.
● High Severity
BNPL Invisible on Product Pages
Klarna and Zippay are both active — confirmed via footer — but zero BNPL messaging appears anywhere on the product page buybox. For a $50–$150 swimwear range, instalment messaging is proven to reduce payment friction and lift add-to-cart rates by 13%+.
▶#9658; Product Pages · Buybox
● High Severity
Zero Trust Signals at Point of Purchase
The cart page shows three trust icons — "Great International Shipping Rates," "Exchanges and Returns," "Call Our Bikini Specialists" — yet none of these appear on the product page where the purchase decision is actually made.
▶#9658; Product Pages · ATC Zone
● Medium Severity
"Sale Price" Label on Every Product — No Actual Discount
All products display "Sale price" as the default price label — even when no real discount exists. This erodes brand trust and dilutes the impact of genuine future promotions, creating confusion for price-aware shoppers.
▶#9658; Product Listings · Cards
